<h2>Using Claude AI in Unreal Engine</h2>

<p>Anthropic's Claude models have earned a reputation for nuanced, thoughtful responses — qualities that translate directly into better game AI. If you want NPCs that feel genuinely intelligent rather than robotic, Claude is worth serious consideration.</p>

<h2>Why Claude for Games?</h2>

<p>Claude excels at following complex instructions and maintaining consistency across long conversations. In a game context, that means:</p>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Characters that stay in character.</strong> Give Claude a detailed persona and it holds it across dozens of exchanges without drifting.</li>
    <li><strong>Nuanced narrative generation.</strong> Claude handles moral ambiguity, emotional subtext, and branching storylines better than most models.</li>
    <li><strong>Extended thinking.</strong> Claude 4.5 and newer models can reason through complex scenarios before responding — perfect for AI game masters that need to consider multiple factors before making narrative decisions.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Claude vs. GPT for Game Development</h2>

<p>Both are excellent. The practical difference comes down to what you're building:</p>

<ul>
    <li>Claude tends to produce more consistent long-form narrative and is better at following detailed system prompts</li>
    <li>GPT models offer a wider ecosystem (TTS, image generation, realtime audio) through a single provider</li>
    <li>Claude's function calling support means it can trigger in-game actions just like OpenAI's models</li>
</ul>

<p>The good news is you don't have to choose one forever.</p>

<h2>How to Integrate</h2>

<p><a href="/t/genai-fab" class="track-click" data-event-name="lnk_clk_genai_fab" data-event-location="post_guide_claude" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">GenAI for Unreal</a> supports Claude alongside OpenAI, Gemini, DeepSeek, and others — all through the same clean API. Switch between providers by changing a single setting, no code rewrite required. The plugin supports Claude Opus 4.6, Sonnet 4.6, and the full Claude 4.5/3.5 family.</p>

<p>This means you can prototype with one model and ship with another, or even let players choose their preferred AI backend.</p>
