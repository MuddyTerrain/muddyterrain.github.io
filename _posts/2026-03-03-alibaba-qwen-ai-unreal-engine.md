<h2>Using Alibaba Qwen AI in Unreal Engine</h2>

<p>Alibaba's Qwen models are among the strongest AI models coming out of China, and for developers targeting Chinese-speaking markets — or anyone who wants access to competitive models with different strengths — they're well worth exploring.</p>

<h2>Why Qwen?</h2>

<p><strong>Excellent Chinese language support.</strong> If your game needs NPCs that speak natural Mandarin, Qwen models are purpose-built for this. The cultural context and language nuance is noticeably better than using western models for Chinese dialogue.</p>

<p><strong>Multimodal capabilities.</strong> Qwen VL models handle both text and images, enabling vision-based gameplay features.</p>

<p><strong>Competitive quality.</strong> Qwen-Max and QVQ-Max regularly benchmark alongside frontier western models. You're not sacrificing intelligence for language support.</p>

<p><strong>Regional availability.</strong> Alibaba offers both Mainland China (Bailian) and international (Singapore Model Studio) endpoints, so the same models work regardless of where your players are.</p>

<h2>Beyond Chat: The Full Stack</h2>

<p>Qwen isn't just a chat model. Through Alibaba's platform you get:</p>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Chat completion</strong> with streaming for real-time dialogue</li>
    <li><strong>Image generation</strong> for dynamic visual content</li>
    <li><strong>Text-to-speech</strong> for voiced NPC dialogue in Chinese</li>
</ul>

<p>That's a complete AI stack for a Chinese-language game, all from one provider.</p>
