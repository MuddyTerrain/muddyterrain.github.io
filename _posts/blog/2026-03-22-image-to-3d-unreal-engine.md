<h2>Image to 3D in Unreal Engine</h2>

<p>Take a 2D image — concept art, a sketch, a photo, or a render — and convert it into a 3D model directly inside Unreal Engine. AI-powered image-to-3D bridges the gap between concept and production, turning art direction into game-ready assets.</p>

<ul>

    <li><strong>Hyper3D Rodin</strong> — 4 quality tiers from fast draft to ultra-detail, up to 300K triangles</li>

<h2>Use Cases</h2>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Concept art pipeline.</strong> Art director sketches a character → AI generates a 3D model → modeler refines. Cuts days off the pipeline.</li>
    <li><strong>Player content.</strong> Players upload images of real objects → game generates 3D versions. Custom items from photos.</li>
    <li><strong>Rapid prototyping.</strong> Screenshot a reference image from the internet → generate a 3D placeholder in minutes.</li>
    <li><strong>Asset variation.</strong> Take one photo, generate 3D models from different providers to compare styles and quality.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Features</h2>

<ul>

    <li>Configurable polygon counts (100 to 300,000 triangles)</li>

</ul>
