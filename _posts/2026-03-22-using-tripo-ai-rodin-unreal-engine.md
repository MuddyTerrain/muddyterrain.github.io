<h2>Using Tripo AI and Hyper3D Rodin in Unreal Engine</h2>

<p>Tripo AI and Hyper3D Rodin are two of the fastest-improving AI 3D generation services. Both offer text-to-3D and image-to-3D with different strengths — Tripo excels at speed and clean topology, while Rodin offers granular quality control with four quality tiers.</p>

<h2>Tripo AI</h2>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Text-to-3D and image-to-3D</strong> with Tripo v3.1</li>
    <li><strong>Fast generation</strong> — results in under a minute for most prompts</li>
    <li><strong>Clean mesh topology</strong> — especially good for organic shapes and characters</li>
    <li><strong>Simple API</strong> — straightforward prompts produce consistent results</li>
</ul>

<h2>Hyper3D Rodin</h2>

<ul>
    <li><strong>4 quality tiers:</strong> Regular, Sketch, Detail, and Smooth — each optimized for different use cases</li>
    <li><strong>4 mesh quality levels:</strong> 500 to 300,000 triangles. From mobile-ready to cinema-quality</li>
    <li><strong>PBR and Shaded material options</strong></li>
    <li><strong>Text-to-3D and image-to-3D</strong></li>
    <li><strong>Fine-grained control</strong> — more parameters than any other provider for precise results</li>
</ul>
